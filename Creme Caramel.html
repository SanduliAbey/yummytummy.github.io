Creme Caramel

Ingredients

250ml granulated sugar, 65ml water, 4 eggs, 500ml milk, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

In a saucepan, combine the sugar and water over medium-low heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves, then increase the heat to medium-high. Allow the mixture to boil without stirring. Swirl the pan occasionally to ensure even caramelization. Cook until the syrup turns a golden brown color. Be careful not to burn it. Once it reaches the desired color, quickly pour the caramel into the bottom of ramekins or a baking dish. Tilt the dish to coat the bottom evenly. Let it cool and harden.In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs until well combined. Add whole milk and vanilla extract. Whisk until smoothes. Strain the custard mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps. Carefully pour the custard mixture over the hardened caramel in the ramekins or baking dish. Place the filled ramekins or baking dish in a larger pan. Fill the larger pan with hot water, creating a water bath that reaches about halfway up the sides of the ramekins. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes or until the custard is set but still slightly jiggly in the center. Then put this into a freezer and let it four about an hour so it will all set. Then you can serve.
