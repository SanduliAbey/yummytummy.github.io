Garlic Bread

Ingredients

bread, 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened 4 cloves garlic, 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper.

Instructions

In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley (if using), salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.Using a serrated knife, slice the Italian bread or baguette diagonally into 1-inch thick slices, but don't cut all the way through the bread. Leave the bottom of the loaf intact so the slices stay connected. Take a generous amount of the garlic butter mixture and spread it onto each slice of bread, ensuring the mixture gets into the cuts. then put it into an oven. serve hot.
